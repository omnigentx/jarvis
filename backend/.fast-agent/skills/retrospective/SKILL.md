# Retrospective Skill

The sprint is complete. Write your retrospective lessons learned.

## Output

Write your retrospective to: `retrospective/<your_agent_name>_lessons.md`

## Template

```markdown
# Retrospective — <Your Agent Name>

## What Went Well
- <Specific things that worked effectively>
- <Reference actual work done, files created, decisions made>

## What Could Be Improved
- <Specific challenges encountered>
- <Things that slowed you down or caused rework>

## Suggestions for the Team
- <Process improvements>
- <Communication improvements>
- <Tool or workflow suggestions>

## Key Learnings
- <Technical insights gained during this sprint>
- <Patterns to reuse in future sprints>
- <Anti-patterns to avoid>

## Action Items for Next Sprint
| # | Action | Owner |
|---|--------|-------|
| 1 | <specific improvement> | <agent name> |
```

## Guidelines

- **Be specific** — reference actual files, decisions, and outcomes from the workspace
- **Be honest** — constructive criticism helps the team improve
- **Be actionable** — every "could be improved" should have a suggestion
- **Read others' work** — check workspace files to understand what the full team accomplished
- **Don't blame** — focus on processes, not people
